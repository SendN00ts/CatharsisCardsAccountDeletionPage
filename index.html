Delete Your Account – Catharsis App

We respect your right to privacy and give you full control over your account data.

To delete your account and all associated data, open the Catharsis app and follow these steps:

  1. Open the Catharsis app on your device.
  2. Tap the Settings icon in the top right.
  3. Select "Delete Account".
  4. Confirm your choice to permanently delete your account and all data.

All your data — including preferences, progress, and saved content — will be permanently deleted from our servers and cannot be recovered.

If you have any questions or run into issues, please contact us at: support@catharsisapp.xyz
